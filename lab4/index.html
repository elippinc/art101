How to wash a pot:

1. Go to the kitchen sink

2. Squeeze a nickle sized amount of dishsoap on a sponge

3. Turn on the faucet to hot

4. Breifly put the soap spondge under the hot water

5. Pick up the pot

6. Put the pot under the running water

7. Turn off the faucet

8. Rub the soap sponge on all dimentions of the pot

9. Scrub until the pot has no dirty spots

10. Turn on the faucet to hot

11. Rinse the pot under the hot water until there is no more dishsoap on it

12. Turn off the faucet

13. Put the wet pot on the drying rack
